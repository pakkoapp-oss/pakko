Privacy Policy

No data collection

Pakko does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data or usage information. All operations are performed locally on your device.

No network access

Pakko does not make any network requests. It has no telemetry, analytics, crash reporting, or update checks. The application operates entirely offline.

Files

Files you choose to compress or extract are processed locally using the operating system's built-in compression APIs. No file content leaves your device.

Third-party services

Pakko does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or advertising networks.

Open source

Pakko is open source and licensed under the Apache License 2.0. You can review the full source code to verify these claims.
